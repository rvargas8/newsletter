The Amazing Octopus

An Informational Text

The octopus is one of the most interesting creatures in the ocean. With its eight long arms, bulging eyes, and ability to change colors, this sea animal looks like something from another planet. Scientists are amazed by how intelligent octopuses are and their many special abilities.

21

One of the octopus's most amazing features is its ability to camouflage itself. When the octopus wants to hide from predators, special cells in its skin can change color to match its surroundings. In seconds, a bright orange octopus can become the same color as the sandy ocean floor or even look like a rock covered in seaweed!

47

Each of the octopus's eight arms is covered with suckers. These suckers allow the octopus to taste and feel whatever it touches. Scientists believe that octopuses might be able to "taste" danger through their arms, helping them decide whether it's safe to grab something or better to swim away.
