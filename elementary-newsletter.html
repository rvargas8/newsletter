Grade Level Guides

Comprehensive information about what families should know for each grade level, aligned with Florida B.E.S.T. Standards

K

Kindergarten

B.E.S.T. ELA Standards

  • Foundational Skills: Letter recognition, letter sounds, and beginning phonics
  • Reading: Recognize and name letters, identify beginning sounds, and read simple words
  • Writing: Write letters and simple words, draw and label pictures
  • Communication: Express ideas orally and participate in conversations
  • Comprehension: Answer questions about stories read aloud

B.E.S.T. Math Standards

  • Counting: Count to 100, count by tens, recognize numbers 0-20
  • Number Sense: Compare quantities, understand more/less/same
  • Operations: Add and subtract within 10 using objects and pictures
  • Geometry: Identify and describe two-dimensional and three-dimensional shapes
  • Measurement: Compare length, weight, and capacity of objects

Florida Standards - Social Studies & Science

  • Social Studies: Learn about families, communities, and basic citizenship
  • Science: Explore the five senses, living and nonliving things, and weather
  • Social Skills: Develop classroom routines, sharing, and following directions
1

First Grade

B.E.S.T. ELA Standards

  • Reading: Decode one-syllable and two-syllable words, read with accuracy and expression
  • Comprehension: Identify main topic, retell stories, and answer questions about text
  • Writing: Write simple sentences with capitalization and punctuation
  • Phonics: Master vowel teams, digraphs, and blends
  • Vocabulary: Use context clues to determine word meanings

B.E.S.T. Math Standards

  • Number Operations: Add and subtract within 20, understand place value (tens and ones)
  • Measurement: Tell time to the hour and half-hour, measure length with non-standard units
  • Data: Organize and interpret data using simple graphs
  • Geometry: Compose and decompose shapes, understand attributes
  • Problem Solving: Solve word problems involving addition and subtraction

Florida Standards - Social Studies & Science

  • Social Studies: Learn about Florida symbols, community roles, and basic geography
  • Science: Study plants and animals, explore Earth materials and patterns in nature
  • Social Skills: Develop independence, conflict resolution, and collaborative learning
2

Second Grade

B.E.S.T. ELA Standards

  • Reading Prose & Poetry: Build fluency and read with expression, identify main idea and story elements
  • Reading Informational Text: Determine main topic and key details in nonfiction texts
  • Writing: Write complete sentences and short paragraphs with clear topic sentences
  • Vocabulary: Use context clues and word relationships to determine meanings
  • Grammar: Identify nouns, verbs, adjectives, and use proper punctuation

B.E.S.T. Math Standards

  • Number Sense: Addition and subtraction within 100, place value (ones, tens, hundreds)
  • Measurement: Tell and write time (analog and digital clocks), measure length
  • Data Analysis: Organize, represent, and interpret data using graphs
  • Money: Identify coins, count money, and solve problems involving money
  • Geometry: Identify and describe two-dimensional shapes

Florida Standards - Social Studies & Science

  • Social Studies: Learn about Florida communities, basic geography, and community helpers
  • Science: Explore life cycles of plants and animals, weather patterns, and seasonal changes
  • Social Skills: Develop independence, peer relationships, and organizational skills
3

Third Grade

B.E.S.T. ELA Standards

  • Reading Prose & Poetry: Determine theme, identify character traits, analyze plot structure
  • Reading Informational Text: Explain author's purpose and point of view, compare texts
  • Writing: Write narrative, opinion, and informational texts with clear organization
  • Vocabulary: Determine meanings of words using affixes, roots, and context clues
  • Research: Conduct short research projects and cite sources

B.E.S.T. Math Standards

  • Number Operations: Master multiplication and division facts (0-12), multi-digit operations
  • Fractions: Understand fractions as parts of a whole, identify equivalent fractions
  • Measurement: Measure and estimate length, capacity, weight, and time
  • Geometry: Understand perimeter and area, classify two-dimensional shapes
  • Data Analysis: Interpret and create bar graphs, pictographs, and line plots

Florida Standards - Critical Transition Year

  • Assessment: Florida FAST (Florida Assessment of Student Thinking) testing begins in 3rd grade
  • Promotion: Third grade promotion depends on meeting reading requirements
  • Social Studies: Study Florida history, state symbols, and civic responsibility
  • Science: Explore forces and motion, habitats, and environmental changes
  • Independence: Develop study skills, time management, and homework routines
4

Fourth Grade

B.E.S.T. ELA Standards

  • Reading Prose & Poetry: Compare and contrast themes, analyze character development
  • Reading Informational Text: Analyze text structure, integrate information from multiple sources
  • Writing: Write opinion pieces with reasons and evidence, multi-paragraph essays
  • Language: Use complex sentences, subject-verb agreement, and proper conventions
  • Research: Conduct research projects using multiple sources and digital tools

B.E.S.T. Math Standards

  • Operations: Multi-digit multiplication and division, word problems with all operations
  • Fractions: Compare, order, add, and subtract fractions with like denominators
  • Decimals: Understand decimal notation, compare and order decimals
  • Measurement: Convert units within measurement systems (length, weight, capacity)
  • Geometry: Classify angles, identify lines of symmetry, understand area and perimeter

Florida Standards - Social Studies & Science

  • Social Studies: Study Florida geography, regions of the United States, and Florida's role in American history
  • Science: Explore forms of energy, weather patterns, Earth's features, and adaptations
  • Academic Skills: Enhanced critical thinking, note-taking, and collaborative project work
  • Assessment: Continue FAST testing with increased rigor and expectations
5

Fifth Grade

B.E.S.T. ELA Standards

  • Reading Prose & Poetry: Analyze themes across texts, interpret figurative language
  • Reading Informational Text: Synthesize information from multiple texts, evaluate author's arguments
  • Writing: Write persuasive essays with clear arguments, research reports, and detailed narratives
  • Language: Master grammar conventions, use academic vocabulary effectively
  • Presentation: Deliver presentations using digital media and public speaking skills

B.E.S.T. Math Standards

  • Fractions & Decimals: Add, subtract, multiply, and divide fractions and mixed numbers
  • Operations: Perform operations with multi-digit decimals, understand percentages
  • Algebraic Thinking: Write and evaluate expressions, identify patterns and relationships
  • Geometry: Calculate volume of rectangular prisms, classify two-dimensional figures
  • Coordinate System: Graph points and interpret data on coordinate planes

Florida Standards - Middle School Preparation

  • Social Studies: Study American history, government structure, and Florida's role in the nation
  • Science: Explore solar system, human body systems, matter and energy
  • Transition Skills: Develop independence, self-advocacy, and responsibility for learning
  • Assessment: Complete FAST assessments preparing for middle school expectations
  • Readiness: Prepare for multiple teachers, class transitions, and increased homework
