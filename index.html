Field Trip Information

Dear Parents & Guardians,

We are excited to announce an upcoming field trip to the Orlando Science Center on Monday, May 4th. Permission slips and a flyer were sent home on Thursday. Below are the important details:

Trip Details

  • Departure from school: 9:00 AM
  • Departure from Orlando Science Center: 4:00 PM
  • Expected arrival at school: 5:00–6:00 PM (we will send an update with the approximate arrival time on the day of the trip)

Lunch & Snacks

  • Students may either pack a lunch from home or request a bagged lunch from the school
  • A form is attached to the permission slip for you to indicate your child’s lunch preference
  • We strongly encourage packing an extra snack, as this will be a full-day outing
  • All lunch and snacks must fit in a clear gallon-sized ziplock bag labeled with your child’s name

Dress Code

  • Students must wear their school uniform or school spirit shirt

What Students Should Know

  • No electronics (phones, tablets, headphones, etc.) are allowed on the bus or during the field trip
  • Students will not be able to purchase anything at the Science Center

Important Actions for Parents

  1. If your child will not be attending: Please contact your child’s homeroom teacher as soon as possible so we can arrange appropriate supervision in another classroom that day
  2. If your child will not attend and will not be at school: Please notify the homeroom teacher so we can accurately track attendance
  3. Chaperone Interest: Chaperone fee is $34 due on MSB once selected by the HR teacher. If you are interested in chaperoning this field trip, please reach out to your child’s homeroom teacher to discuss availability

Questions? Please email your child’s homeroom teacher with any additional questions or concerns.

We look forward to a wonderful learning experience at the Orlando Science Center!
