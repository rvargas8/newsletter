FAST Test Prep 📝

We are officially entering test prep season! We will be reviewing for the Reading and Math FAST test this week. These review days are extremely important for helping students refresh and strengthen their skills before testing. Please make every effort to have your child at school each day during this time.

As we prepare for the upcoming FAST assessments, we wanted to share a helpful resource with you. The FAST website offers practice tests that your child can use at home to become more familiar with the format and types of questions they will see. Practicing is completely optional, but it can help students feel more confident and comfortable on test day.

How to Access FAST Practice Tests:

  1. Go to https://flfast.org/
  2. Click Florida Statewide Assessments
  3. Select Students and Families
  4. Click View Sample Items for Grades 3–10
  5. Choose Continue as Guest
  6. Click Continue in a Guest Session
  7. Select Third Grade, then Sample Test
  8. Choose either the Reading or Math sample test and continue from there

Thank you so much for supporting your child's learning at home! 💙
