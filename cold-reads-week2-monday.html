The Mysterious Cave

An Adventure Story

Emma and her brother Jake loved exploring the woods behind their house. One Saturday morning, they discovered something they had never seen before—a dark opening in the side of the hill. It was a cave!

15

"Should we go in?" Jake asked nervously. Emma pulled out her flashlight and smiled. "We came prepared for an adventure, didn't we?" She turned on her flashlight and carefully stepped inside.

30

The cave was cool and damp. Water dripped from the ceiling, making soft splashing sounds. Their flashlight beams danced across the rocky walls, revealing sparkling crystals embedded in the stone. "Look at those!" Emma gasped. The crystals looked like tiny stars frozen in the rock.

As they ventured deeper, Jake noticed something unusual. "Emma, there are marks on the wall." They looked closer and saw old drawings—pictures of animals and people. "These must be really old," Emma whispered. "Maybe people lived here a long time ago."
