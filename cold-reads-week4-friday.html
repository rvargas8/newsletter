How Plants Make Food

A Persuasive Text

You should plant a garden because plants help make our world beautiful and healthy! Plants do something amazing called photosynthesis, where they use sunlight, water, and air to make their own food. This amazing process helps clean the air we breathe.

20

When you have plants in your yard, they provide homes for birds, butterflies, and bees. These animals help pollinate flowers, which helps plants grow fruits and vegetables. Without plants, we wouldn't have the food we need to survive.

41

Plants also make our neighborhoods look beautiful. Green grass, colorful flowers, and tall trees make our world a more pleasant place to live. When you plant a tree, you're helping to clean the air because trees absorb carbon dioxide and give us fresh oxygen to breathe.

66

Maybe you're thinking that gardening is a lot of work. But actually, starting small is easy! You can begin with just a few flowers or herbs. Plus, taking care of plants teaches you responsibility and patience. Watching something you planted grow and bloom is one of the most satisfying feelings in the world.

93

So next time you have a chance, plant something! Every seed you plant helps make our world a little bit better. You'll be helping animals, cleaning the air, and making your community more beautiful. Start with one small plant and see how it changes your world for the better.
