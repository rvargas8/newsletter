Sam's Fast Ride

Historical Fiction - Nevada, 1860

Sam held tight to his horse as they raced across the desert. He was eighteen years old and worked for the Pony Express. His job was to carry mail from one town to another. It was dangerous work, but Sam loved it.

Sam reached the first station. A fresh horse was waiting for him. Sam jumped off his tired horse and onto the new one. He only had two minutes! An old man named Tom grabbed Sam's arm. "Be careful," Tom said. "There might be trouble up ahead."

Sam nodded. He knew the land belonged to Native American tribes before settlers came. Some tribes were angry that so many people were taking their land. Sam understood why they were upset, but he had mail to deliver.

Dark clouds covered the sky. Soon it started to rain. The ground became muddy and slippery. But Sam kept going. The mail had to get through! Maybe someone was waiting for an important letter.
